The campus employee time keeping system, Kronos.berkeley.edu, has scheduling capabilities, but it lacks important tools for what our student employees need, especially the key abilities to quickly post a weekly schedule, post availabilities(including classes other obligations, preferred times, rather not work times, etc.), easily swap shifts, and make time-off requests.

  • It should allow employees to enter their availabilities and time off requests. Drag and drop blocks to create schedules. Preferred, School, Rather not work, Other Obligations, and blank.
  • All employee availabilities viewed by the scheduling staff and supervisors.
  • Admin - Ability to create different scheduling periods for schedules of various lengths, i.e. Spring 2012, Spring Break 2012 Finals Week 1, Finals Week 2, Summer 2012, etc.
  • The software should be able stack all the availabilities so to list all people who can work at a particular hour(9AM DC DB AF ER are available to work) . Also color code to show those available to work also at that hour also have that hour as preferred, empty, or rather not work.
  • Based on availabilities, an interface to drag and drop people into shifts to create a weekly schedule and automatically email out to staff.

Scheduling part

  • place people and facilities into units/divisions and be able to rearrange them and add as needed. Also the ability to block people from taking subs from certain units/division if needed.
  • The ability to allow people to view shifts, sub out shifts, take shifts, enter in shifts not on the systems(manual substitutions, time edits, etc.). Application should email out when subs are posted, taken, canceled , etc. When time edits are entered , they should only be emailed to supervisors and schedulers.
  • A utility to count up all shifted and non-shifted hours output to a Kronos looking page so people can use to enter their hours into Kronos.
  • Admin side-Ability to take subs for employees, post subs for employees, xx shifts, cancel subs, view all time edits and add up hours.
  • Ability to output employee information(shifts, contact info, etc.) for modules in Drupal or small scripts for web pages, i.e http://cafe.berkeley.edu/sscripts/wode
  • Robust Admin area to manage new student employees and facilities
  • CAS authentication for the entire application
